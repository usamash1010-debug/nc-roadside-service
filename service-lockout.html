/ FAQ

Common questions

Quick answers about lockout service in the Charlotte metro.

Will you damage my car getting in?

No. We use proper lockout tools designed to open vehicles without damaging the door, glass or paint — not coat hangers or pry bars.

There's a child locked in the car — what do I do?

Call us immediately and tell the dispatcher. We prioritize these calls. If anyone is in danger, call 911 first; otherwise stay with the vehicle and we'll get there fast.

Can you make a new key if mine is lost?

Lockout service gets you back into the vehicle. For lost keys or fob programming, we'll get you in and advise on the best next step for a replacement key.
