/ FAQ

Common questions

Quick answers about flat tire change in the Charlotte metro.

What if I don't have a spare tire?

No problem — many newer vehicles don't come with one. We offer mobile tire repair (plug/patch) and can source a replacement tire, so you may not need a tow at all.

Can you change a tire on the interstate?

Yes, and we do it safely with the proper setup. Stay buckled inside your vehicle, well off the travel lanes, and let us handle the roadside work when we arrive.

Will you torque the wheel properly?

Always. We torque lug nuts to manufacturer spec rather than just snugging them by hand, which is critical for safety at speed.
