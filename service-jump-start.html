/ FAQ

Common questions

Quick answers about jump starts & battery in the Charlotte metro.

How fast can you get to me for a jump start?

In most of the Charlotte metro our average response time is around 30 minutes. Call our 24/7 line and we'll give you a real ETA based on your exact location.

Do I need a new battery or just a jump?

That's exactly what we check. After we get you running, we test the battery and charging system. If it tests healthy you're good to go; if it's failing, we'll tell you straight and can usually replace it on the spot.

Can you jump a battery in a parking deck or garage?

Yes. We regularly handle jump starts in parking decks, driveways, lots and on the shoulder. Just give us the level and nearest entrance when you call.
