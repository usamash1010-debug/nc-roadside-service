/ OUR STORY

Built on the roads we serve.

NC Roadside Service started with a simple frustration: too many drivers were left waiting hours for help that never felt local. We set out to do it differently — real people, real trucks, and a dispatch line that actually answers.

Based in Belmont and working across the greater Charlotte metro, we've built our reputation one shoulder, parking lot, and late-night call at a time. Cars, trucks, and growing business fleets all count on us to get them moving.

We're licensed, fully insured, and available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year — because breakdowns don't keep business hours.
