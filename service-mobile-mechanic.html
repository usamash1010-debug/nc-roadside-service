/ FAQ

Common questions

Quick answers about mobile mechanic in the Charlotte metro.

What kind of repairs can you do roadside?

Minor, safe-to-perform repairs and adjustments — things like battery and connection issues, simple swaps, and diagnostics. Major mechanical work still needs a shop, and we'll tell you when that's the case.

How is this different from a tow?

A tow just moves your vehicle. Our mobile mechanic tries to actually fix the issue on the spot, which can save you the cost and hassle of a tow and a shop visit.

Can you tell me if it's worth fixing roadside or towing?

Yes — that honest assessment is the whole point. We diagnose first, then recommend the option that actually makes sense for your situation.
