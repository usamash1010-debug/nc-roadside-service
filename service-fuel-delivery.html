/ OVERVIEW

Fuel Delivery

Running out of fuel leaves you stranded in some of the worst possible spots — a highway shoulder, a busy intersection, or miles from the nearest station. We deliver gas or diesel directly to your vehicle, enough to get you safely to a pump.

It's faster and far safer than hiking along a road with a can and hoping a station is nearby. One call and we're on the way.

Fuel delivery pairs naturally with our other services, so if your no-start turns out to be something other than an empty tank, our tech is already there to help figure it out.

/ FAQ

Common questions

Quick answers about fuel delivery in the Charlotte metro.

How much fuel do you bring?

Enough to get you safely to the nearest fueling station. We don't fill your whole tank roadside, but you'll have plenty to reach a pump.

Do you deliver diesel too?

Yes. Let us know when you call whether you need gas or diesel and we'll bring the right fuel for your vehicle.

My car still won't start after fuel — now what?

Our tech is already on-site, so we'll help diagnose it. Sometimes it's the battery or starter rather than the tank, and we can address that too or advise on next steps.
