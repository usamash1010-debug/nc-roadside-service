/ FAQ

Common questions

Quick answers about mobile tire repair in the Charlotte metro.

Can every flat tire be repaired?

No — punctures in the tread can usually be plugged or patched, but sidewall damage or large holes mean the tire needs replacing for safety. We'll tell you which it is.

Do you carry replacement tires with you?

We source the right replacement for your vehicle. Give us your tire size (on the sidewall) when you call so we can come prepared.

Is a mobile repair as good as a shop repair?

For repairable tread punctures, yes — we use proper plug/patch methods. The difference is we do it at your location instead of you waiting at a shop.
